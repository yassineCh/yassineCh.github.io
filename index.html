Research Interests

My work advances offline decision-making, learning to optimize and act safely from logged data when new experiments or interactions are costly. I build methods that turn static datasets into effective search and control policies for high-stakes domains such as materials discovery, healthcare, and smart grids.

  • Offline Model-Based Optimization: I develop policy-guided search and surrogate modeling for expensive black-box design. Our Policy-Guided Gradient Search reframes offline optimization as offline RL to learn how to move in design space, improving over fixed surrogate-only strategies, and our search-bias-guided surrogates discover better nanoporous materials by aligning the model’s ranking with the optimization goal.   • Offline Safe Reinforcement Learning: I design safety-aware offline RL that works under tight or changing constraints. For fixed budgets, we cast OSRL as a minimax problem and solve it efficiently with a multi-armed-bandit approximation that enforces costs while preserving reward, and for variable test-time limits, CAPS learns multiple policies offline and switches among them at deployment to satisfy new constraints without retraining.
