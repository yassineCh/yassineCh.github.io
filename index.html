I am a PhD student at Washington State University, advised by Prof. Jana Doppa.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in research on Offline Optimization and Reinforcement Learning. My current research focuses primarily on:

  • Offline Model-Based Optimization: Developing surrogate models and efficient strategies for solving high-dimensional design problems.
  • Offline Safe Reinforcement Learning: Ensuring safety constraints, robust policy optimization with limited data, and risk-aware decision-making.
